DCMA Notice and Takedown Policy

Rubick Cloud Music respects and protects all the users' IP and creative ideas. We do not accept any styles of copy-and-paste, cheating, and stealing during the time you using our Web Application. Below is the specification of our DCMA policy:

DCMA Declearation

Users could write their own reviews, ideas and opinions about the posted musics. However, we DO NOT allow the following actions during the using process:

  • Literally copy others' reviews and comments and post again.
  • Intentially post same general comments for different songs to bother others.
  • Steal others' reviews from other websites without mentioning the real author.

Takedown Policy

Once your account/comment/reviews got reported from other users, the administrator will check and review the report message. If the cheating has been verified, you might face the following punishment:

  • Your post will be removed immediately.
  • Your account will get banned (deactivated) for 24 hours.
  • You might face a fine of stealing others IP according to how bad the effect of your action caused.
